Eswatini officially the Kingdom of Eswatini since 19 April 2018, is a landlocked sovereign state in Southern Africa.

Swaziland is one of the smallest countries in Africa.

Despite this, its climate and topography are diverse, ranging from a cool and mountainous highveld to a hot and dry lowveld. The population is primarily ethnic Swazis, whose language is siSwati.

Incwala

Incwala is the main ritual of Kingship in the Kingdom of Eswati.

This is a national event that takes place during the summer solstice.

Umhlanga (Reed Dance)

Umhlanga is an annual Swazi and Zulu event.

In Eswatii, tens of thousands of unmarried and childless Swazi girls and women travel from the various chiefdoms to the Ludzidzini Royal Village to participate in the eight-day event.

Marula

The Marula season begins each year in mid February and continues until May, bringing with it a celebration of the harvest of the marula fruit.

The fruits are then placed into water, sugar is added and it is fermented, and distilled into a beer. This potent alcoholic mixture is called buganu, or marula beer.
